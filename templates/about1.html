Intern Predict

Overview: InternPredict is a predictive analytics tool designed to assist organizations in making informed decisions regarding their intern management processes. The tool leverages data-driven insights to predict outcomes in three critical areas:

  • Attrition: Predict the likelihood of an intern leaving the organization.
  • Placement: Assess the likelihood of offering a full-time position to an intern.
  • Lead Conversion: Predict the success rate of converting potential intern candidates into actual interns.

It is developed using Python and deployed as a web application using the Flask framework, making it accessible, lightweight, and easy to integrate into organizational workflows.

Modules and Their Functions:

Attrition Prediction:

  • Purpose: Predict the likelihood of an intern leaving before completion.
  • Use Case: Helps HR identify at-risk interns and take proactive measures.
  • Inputs: Attendance, project performance, feedback, and engagement levels.
  • Output: A prediction score indicating attrition risk with actionable insights.

Placement Prediction:

  • Purpose: Assess the likelihood of a full-time offer for an intern.
  • Use Case: Streamlines recruitment processes with data-driven decisions.
  • Inputs: Project deliverables, skills assessments, and feedback.
  • Output: A placement probability score with recommendations.

Lead Conversion Prediction:

  • Purpose: Predict success rates for converting potential candidates to interns.
  • Use Case: Optimizes recruitment strategy for high-potential leads.
  • Inputs: Academic scores, achievements, and interview performance.
  • Output: A conversion likelihood score with actionable insights.
